About SINOX

SINOX is a professional manufacturer of memory modules and solid-state drives. Founded with a vision to deliver reliable, high-performance storage solutions, we have grown into a trusted brand serving customers in over 50 countries worldwide.

Our core team has been engaged in the storage industry since 2008, accumulating deep expertise in DRAM ICs, DDR3/DDR4/DDR5 memory modules, and SSD technology. We operate R&D and manufacturing facilities in Shenzhen, with a commitment to ISO 9001 quality management standards.
